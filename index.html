CSAT Trend Commentary

üìä Overall CSAT: 56% ‚Üí 66% +10pts

Overall CSAT initially dipped to 52% in May 2025 during the gen migration ‚Äî expected as customers encountered a fundamentally different experience. It recovered quickly and has been on a consistent upward trajectory, surpassing the scripted-era peak of 56% by August and reaching 66% by January 2026. The improvement is driven by the AI Agent's natural language ability, better handling of multi-intent queries, and reduction in frustrating dead-end loops. Target: 70%+ in 2026.

üë§ Agent CSAT: 62% ‚Üí 82% Stable

Human agent CSAT has been stable at ~82% since the Engage improvements in 2022 (up from 62%). This serves as the benchmark target for the AI Agent. The gap between agent and AI Agent CSAT has been narrowing: it was 45pts at gen go-live (82% vs 37%) and is now only 19pts (82% vs 63%). If the AI Agent continues its current trajectory of ~3pts/month, it could approach parity by mid-2026.

ü§ñ AI Agent CSAT: 37% ‚Üí 63% +26pts

The most dramatic improvement. AI Agent CSAT plummeted to 37% at launch ‚Äî a known risk during gen migration as the model calibrates to iHerb's complex, multi-language, API-heavy use cases. The recovery has been remarkable: +26pts in 8 months. Key drivers include coaching refinements, playbook adherence improvements, language-switching bug fixes, and the team's intensive work on handoff quality. Eric's execs reframed CSAT expectations during this period. Target: 75% in 2026.

üí° Why CSAT Dipped at Launch ‚Äî And Why It's Normal

The initial CSAT dip is a well-documented pattern during scripted ‚Üí generative migrations. Scripted bots score higher on CSAT for simple, predictable flows because customers know what to expect. Generative bots initially score lower because they attempt to resolve more complex issues (which would have been immediately handed off before), and customers hold them to a higher conversational standard. As the AI Agent improves through coaching, knowledge refinement, and playbook optimization, CSAT recovers and ultimately exceeds scripted levels ‚Äî exactly what we see with iHerb.
